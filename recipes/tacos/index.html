Tacos

January 16, 2022 | Suggest Changes
Ingredients
Required
taco shells, hard or soft
spice mix
cheese
Filling options
vegan ground meat + seasoning
black beans
salsa
butternut squash
red cabbage

Instructions#

  1. Roas the vegetables in the oven with desired spices
  2. Stir fry the meat with taco seasoning
  3. Beans can be used either
  • straight from the can
  • or crushed and fried with spices
  1. Assemble tacos on-demand
